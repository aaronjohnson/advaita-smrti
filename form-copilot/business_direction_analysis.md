## The Hybrid Approach

Many successful self-employed people combine multiple directions:

**Primary (income focus):**
- Choose the option with faster path to revenue
- This pays the bills while you build

**Secondary (growth focus):**
- Choose the option that builds long-term value
- This could become marketing for your primary
- Or transition to primary once established

**Synergy questions:**
- Can work from Direction 1 become content/case studies for Direction 2?
- Can Direction 2 attract clients for Direction 1?
- Do both benefit from the same core skills?

## Questions to Consider

1. **Financial pressure:** How soon do you need income? Can you afford a slow ramp-up?

2. **Energy and structure:** Do you need external accountability (clients) or can you self-direct?

3. **Social interaction:** How much client contact do you want?

4. **Risk tolerance:** Which path has less downside risk?

5. **Long-term vision:** Where do you want to be in 5 years?
