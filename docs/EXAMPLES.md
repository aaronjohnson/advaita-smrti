# Example Configs

Four example configs spanning business, academic, creative, and entrepreneurial applications.

On first run, you'll be prompted to choose which form to use. Switch forms anytime from the menu.

## Included Examples

| File | Questions | Use Case |
|------|-----------|----------|
| `oregon_sea_config.json` | 49 | Self-employment assistance, business planning |
| `college_app_config.json` | 17 | College essays, Common App, supplementals |
| `creative_grant_config.json` | 22 | Artist grants, creative project proposals |
| `product_concept_config.json` | 44 | Product pitches, startup planning, idea validation |

## Oregon SEA Application

**49 questions** covering the Oregon Self-Employment Assistance program requirements:
- Business overview and viability
- Market analysis and competition
- Financial projections
- Personal qualifications
- Implementation timeline

Best for: State unemployment programs, small business grants, self-employment proposals.

## College Application Essays

**17 questions** covering typical college application requirements:
- Personal statement / Common App essay
- Activity descriptions
- "Why this school" supplementals
- Short answer questions

Best for: College apps, graduate school applications, scholarship essays.

## Creative Grant Application

**22 questions** for artist and creative project funding:
- Project description and vision
- Artistic background
- Community impact
- Budget and timeline
- Work samples context

Best for: Arts council grants, foundation funding, residency applications.

## Product Concept Discovery

**44 questions** structured for developing a product pitch:
- Problem space and validation
- Target users and market size
- Solution and competitive landscape
- Business model and go-to-market
- MVP scope and technical approach
- Team, resources, and risks

Best for: Startup pitches, product planning, idea validation, investor presentations.

## Creating Your Own

See [CONFIG.md](CONFIG.md) for detailed instructions on creating custom configs.

The fastest way: paste your application questions into Claude and use `/generate-config`.
