────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

OPENING THE CENTER

  1.  Turn on the client kiosk.
      It starts automatically. No login needed.
      The check-in screen appears within 30 seconds.

  2.  Turn on the staff station (if separate device).
      Log in with your staff username and password.

  3.  Verify the date shown on the kiosk is correct.

  That's it. The system is ready for clients.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

DURING THE DAY

  Clients:
  - Walk up to the kiosk
  - Enter their client code (card number) or name
  - Tap "Check In"
  - Select services (meal, shower, laundry, etc.)
  - Done

  Staff:
  - New clients: register on the staff station
  - Issue a client code card to the new client
  - Look up clients, edit records, add notes
  - Check-out is automatic at closing or manual

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

END OF DAY: EXPORTING DATA

  When you need to pull a report or create a backup:

  Step 1.  On the staff station, go to Reports.

  Step 2.  Select the report type:
           - Daily check-in count
           - Monthly summary
           - Client list
           - HMIS export (if applicable)

  Step 3.  Insert the USB flash drive into the
           staff station.

  Step 4.  Tap "Export to USB."

  Step 5.  Enter your staff password when prompted.
           (This authorizes the export and logs it.)

  Step 6.  Wait for the green checkmark.

  Step 7.  Remove the USB drive.

  The USB drive now contains:
  - Your requested report(s) as CSV/Excel files
  - These files are NOT encrypted (they are meant
    to be opened in Excel on any computer)
  - The daily encrypted backup (runs automatically)

  IMPORTANT: The exported CSV/Excel files contain
  client information. Handle the USB drive with the
  same care as paper client records. Do not leave it
  unattended. Return it to the locked drawer/cabinet
  when not in use.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

WEEKLY: BACKUP VERIFICATION

  Once per week (suggested: Friday before closing):

  1.  On the staff station, go to Settings > Backup.
  2.  Tap "Run Backup Now."
  3.  Wait for confirmation: "Backup complete."
  4.  The backup is written to the USB drive as an
      encrypted file. Even if the USB drive is lost,
      the data cannot be read without the system
      password.
  5.  Store the USB drive in a secure location
      (locked drawer or cabinet).

  Keep at least two USB drives in rotation:
  - Drive A stays in secure storage as last week's backup.
  - Drive B is the active drive used this week.
  - Swap them each week.

────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

IF SOMETHING GOES WRONG

  Kiosk screen is blank or frozen:
  - Unplug the power cable, wait 10 seconds, plug
    it back in. The kiosk restarts automatically.
  - No data is lost. Check-in records are saved
    continuously.

  "Database locked" or error message:
  - Restart the kiosk (unplug/replug).
  - If the error persists after restart, contact
    technical support.

  Forgot staff password:
  - An administrator can reset your password from
    the staff station under Settings > Users.
  - If no administrator is available, contact
    technical support.

  USB drive is full:
  - Delete old export files from the USB drive
    using any computer.
  - Or use a new USB drive.

  USB drive is lost:
  - The encrypted backup on the lost drive cannot
    be read by anyone without the system password.
  - Use the second rotation drive to continue
    operations.
  - Report the loss per your organization's data
    incident policy.
